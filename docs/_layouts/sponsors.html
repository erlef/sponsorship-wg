How sponsorship works

A fair-share contribution.

Sponsorship scales with your organization's revenue, so a small consultancy and a global enterprise both contribute proportionally. The amounts below are the minimum at each tier — sponsors are welcome and encouraged to give more.

Annual revenue Minimum annual contribution
Under $1,000,000 $2,000or more
$1,000,000 – $5,000,000 $5,000or more
$5,000,000+ $10,000or more
Become a Sponsor →

Many sponsors give above the minimum to fund specific programs. Custom arrangements and in-kind support also welcome — get in touch.

Where your contribution goes

One pool. Shared impact.

  • Foundation operations & governance

Common questions

Is my contribution tax-deductible?

The Erlang Ecosystem Foundation is a 501(c)(3) registered in the United States. Contributions are tax-deductible where applicable; treatment varies by jurisdiction.

How do I become a sponsor?

Email sponsorship@erlef.org. We'll confirm your tier and onboard you.

Can we sponsor a specific Working Group?

Yes — earmarked contributions above the minimum can be directed to a specific WG or program. Mention this when you reach out.

Where does the money go?

Working Group programs, grants, events, and operations. See our public records for the full breakdown.
